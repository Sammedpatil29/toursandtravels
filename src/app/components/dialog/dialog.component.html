Terms and Conditions

  • Advance Payment:
    • An advance payment is required to hold the booking for the package.
    • The balance amount should be paid 20 days before the date of the journey.
  • Domestic Group Packages:
    • Rs. 20,000/- per person for Domestic group packages.
  • Payment Methods:
    • Advance/full payment can be made via:
      • Online Payment
      • Bank Deposit
      • Cash or Cheque at the office or to the executive.
  • Hotel Accommodation:
    • Hotels will be provided according to the package itinerary.
  • Children Policy:
    • Children below 5 years: No charge (no seats provided).
    • Children aged 5 to 10 years: 70% of the package cost is applicable (extra mattresses will be provided in the room).
  • Conveyance Charges:
    • Any additional conveyance charges from the bus alighting point to mountains, hotels, or other locations where the tourist bus cannot go due to traffic regulations or conditions are to be borne by the tourists.
  • Sightseeing & Meal Refund:
    • No refund will be provided for missed sightseeing tours or unused meals (including complimentary meals).
  • Triple Sharing:
    • In case of triple sharing (e.g., a couple with a mother), extra mattresses will be provided in the same room. Room heater charges and other incidentals are extra.
  • Personal Belongings:
    • Passengers are requested to take care of their personal belongings.
    • The management is not responsible for any damage, theft, or loss of items left in the coach or handed over to staff.
  • Vehicle Change:
    • Depending on the number of people in the tour, the vehicle type may be changed from a bus to a smaller vehicle (e.g., Tempo Traveler, Toyota, Car, etc.).
  • Seating Arrangement for Bus Packages:
    • For bus packages, seating will be arranged on a "Dip and Daily Seat Rotation" basis.
  • Minimum Batch Requirement:
    • If a minimum batch size is not met for any package, the scheduled date may be changed or an alternate vehicle may be arranged as per bookings.
  • No Responsibility for Personal Belongings:
    • Tour managers and company staff are not responsible for any personal belongings such as cameras, mobiles, or ornaments.
  • Medical Report for Tourists Above 65:
    • Tourists above the age of 65 years are required to submit a medical report signed by an MD doctor, which should not be older than 30 days.
  • Health Insurance:
    • Health insurance is mandatory for all travelers, covering the entire travel period.
    • SRI MAHAADEV TOURS & TRAVELS is not responsible for health-related issues during the tour.
  • Final Itinerary:
    • The final itinerary will be provided two days before the travel date.
